Overview

We investigate how image similarity metrics can be leveraged to automate the adjustment of label colors and styles in augmented reality (AR) applications. Specifically, I’m experimenting with metrics such as Learned Perceptual Image Patch Similarity (LPIPS) and Structural Similarity Index Measure (SSIM) to create more high-contrast and human-readable labels in AR scenes, and to eventually automate the process by integrating them into a neural network. Through this project, I’m hoping to explore color-perception mechanisms and refine perceptual image similarity models in order to provide users with an extra layer of easily accessible information, ultimately enhancing the AR experience.
